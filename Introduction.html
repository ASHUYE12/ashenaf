Introduction

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a modern technology where computer machines mimic human behavior, specifically learning from experience and adjusting to new inputs. For the most part, the current AI depends on deep learning to simulate the human experience, with the AI devices possessing the ability to process large amounts of data as well as identifying patterns in data fed to them. The benefits of AI cut across industries, with almost all economic sector identifying specific uses of the technology. The government has not been left behind. Apart from the government embracing AI, it is working towards laying down the necessary legislative infrastructure for the seamless rollout of the technology.
